Behind-the-scenes

This project was filmed using three Sony cameras — two A7 IV units and one FX3, paired with Tamron 17–28mm, Sony 24–70mm GM, and Tamron 28–75mm lenses.

The shoot took place at night inside Van Hanh Mall, during a large-scale event featuring celebrity guests Quang Hùng MasterD, Ninh Anh Bùi, and Nguyễn Tùng Dương. The environment was dynamic and fast-paced, requiring flexible setups and precise coordination between all three cameras. Lighting was achieved using two Nanlite 300W fixtures combined with stage and event lighting already present at the venue. The FX3 handled low-light close-ups with cinematic depth, while the A7 IV units covered wide and mid-range angles to capture both the crowd and performance details. With Sony’s strong color science, impressive low-light performance, and reliable stabilization, the production maintained clarity and mood across every frame — reflecting the energy and excitement of the night.
