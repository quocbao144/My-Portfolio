Behind-the-scenes

This project is a film dedicated to capturing the authentic and intimate moments of a wedding ceremony held at the family home. The entire video was filmed on the Sony A74 with a 28-75mm lens and a gimbal, relying completely on natural light.

Set within the familiar and warm environment of the home, the production focused on capturing the most genuine and heartfelt emotions. The natural light streaming through the windows not only provided true-to-life colors but also created a soft, tender atmosphere, perfectly highlighting the happiness and connection between the couple and their families. The combination of the versatile 28-75mm lens and the fluidity of the gimbal allowed the videographer to move discreetly and gracefully through the space. This enabled the capture of cherished gestures, exchanged glances, and radiant smiles in the most natural way possible, without disrupting the solemnity of the ceremony. The final result is an emotional, elegant, and sincere film that preserves the precious memories of the couple's special day in a close, intimate, and warm-hearted manner.
