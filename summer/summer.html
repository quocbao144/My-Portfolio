Behind-the-scenes

This project was filmed using two Sony A6700 cameras.

A 18–135mm lens was used to capture detailed action shots during the model's performance of specific movements, while a 24–70mm lens was employed for medium and wide-angle framing.Unlike controlled studio lighting, this shoot relied entirely on natural light to preserve the authentic atmosphere of the location. The scenes were filmed during early morning and late afternoon, taking advantage of the soft golden sunlight to highlight textures and depth without artificial illumination. The positioning of the model and camera was carefully adjusted to maximize the direction and quality of ambient light, creating subtle contrasts and a warm, organic tone throughout the footage. To maintain visual consistency, white balance and exposure settings were manually controlled on both cameras, ensuring smooth color transitions across shots while preserving the natural look and mood of the environment.
