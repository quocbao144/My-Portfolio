Behind-the-scenes

This spot was shot on the Lumix S1H coupled with the Leica R set.

The production consists of 2 sections: studio and outdoors. While the studio setup was much more controlled – we lit it with simply 2 Aputure 1200x’s outside, and an Amaran F22c inside, the whole production generally has a very documentary style to it. The focus was more on capturing the moments rather than grabbing the exact shot in post. The S1H, being a relatively old camera, shined in these situations. Stunning Lumix color science, unbelievable in-body stabilization, and great dynamic range, packed in small mirrorless body, all contributed to how we managed to shoot this.
