Behind-the-scenes

This project was filmed on the Sony A6700, paired with a set of 18–135mm, 24–70mm, and 70–180mm lenses.

The production took place entirely in the mountainous forests of Da Lat, focusing on natural landscapes and wildlife elements. Filming was conducted from early morning until late afternoon, relying solely on natural light to capture the ever-changing atmosphere — from soft morning haze to the golden glow of sunset. Each lens played a specific role: the 18–135mm for agile handheld shots and movement tracking, the 24–70mm for balanced medium compositions, and the 70–180mm for isolating distant subjects with cinematic compression. The A6700’s excellent color science, impressive dynamic range, and reliable in-body stabilization allowed the entire shoot to feel organic and fluid, even in challenging terrain. The result is a collection of visuals that reflect the serenity, depth, and raw beauty of nature in its purest form.
