Behind-the-scenes

This project was filmed using the Sony A6700, paired with 18–135mm and 24–70mm lenses, along with an ND filter to balance exposure under natural sunlight

The shoot focused on outdoor floral scenes, capturing the delicate textures and colors of flowers in their natural environment. Filming took place entirely under natural light, allowing the true tones of the petals and surrounding greenery to shine through without artificial enhancement. The 18–135mm lens provided flexibility for wider compositions and detailed close-ups, while the 24–70mm offered a cinematic mid-range look with smooth background separation. The ND filter helped maintain consistent contrast and highlight control during bright daylight conditions, resulting in footage that feels organic, balanced, and true to nature.
