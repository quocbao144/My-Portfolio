Behind-the-scenes

This project was filmed on the Sony A6700, using 35mm and 18–135mm lenses, paired with an ND filter to maintain natural exposure under available light.

Shot handheld inside Đậm Đà Lạt Café, the film captures the process of crafting beverages — from the subtle motions of pouring coffee to the textures of foam and glass — all within the cozy, organic atmosphere of the space. The production relied entirely on natural light, allowing soft highlights and gentle shadows to shape the mood authentically. The 35mm lens brought intimacy to close-up shots of the barista’s hands and ingredients, while the 18–135mm provided dynamic framing for environmental details and candid café moments.
