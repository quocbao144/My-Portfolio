Behind-the-scenes

This project is a professional multi-camera production capturing a live performance of the song "Chăm em một đời" by singer Đức Phúc on the grand stage at Vạn Phúc City. The production utilized a setup of two Sony FX3 cameras and one Sony A74, paired with a lens arsenal including the 24-70mm GM, 28-75mm, 100-400mm, and a flycam.

Set against the backdrop of a vibrant evening event, the production skillfully leveraged the dynamic stage lighting to create a visually stunning and emotional atmosphere. The three-camera setup allowed for comprehensive coverage of the performance from every angle: The two primary cameras (Sony FX3s), equipped with the 24-70mm GM and 28-75mm lenses, captured the essential wide and medium shots, focusing on Đức Phúc's choreography, stage presence, and overall performance. The Sony A74, paired with the 100-400mm telephoto lens, was positioned at a distance, dedicated to capturing tight, cinematic close-ups that highlighted the singer's heartfelt expressions and subtle details. The flycam was strategically deployed for the intro and outro, delivering breathtaking aerial shots that showcased the immense scale of the stage, the massive audience, and the glittering cityscape of Vạn Phúc City at night. The final product is a dynamic and professional music video with diverse perspectives and crisp image quality, even in low-light conditions. It successfully conveys the full energy and emotion of the song while capturing the grandeur of the live event.
